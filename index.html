Questions, answered

Frequently asked.

Do I need experience to join?

Not at all. Many of our strongest competitors started knowing nothing about their event. Captains and coaches help you learn from the ground up.

How much of a time commitment is it?

It scales with you — a weekly meeting plus independent study or build time with your partner. More as a tournament approaches, but you control the pace.

What's the difference between build and study events?

Build events (Boomilever, Helicopter) mean designing and constructing a device. Study events (Astronomy, Forensics) are knowledge- and lab-based. Most members do a mix.

How many events can I do?

Typically 3–4 events per competitor, each with 1–2 partners. We help you balance your schedule so events don't conflict at tournaments.

When can I join?

We recruit heavily at the start of the school year, but reach out any time — email westviewso@gmail.com.
